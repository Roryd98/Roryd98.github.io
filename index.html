The Day

Timeline

Here's the shape of our day, we hope it helps you settle in and enjoy every moment with us.

Saturday 19th September — 12:00 noon

The Ceremony

St John's Church, Moira. Please be seated by 11:45am. The ceremony will last approximately 45 minutes and will include readings, vows, and a blessing. We kindly ask that phones are on silent and no photos are taken during the ceremony.

Please make your way to Lough Erne where there will be canapés and refreshments on arrival.

3:30 pm

Drinks Reception

Join us at Lough Erne Resort, The Ross Suite, for a welcome drink and canapés, with live music.

5:45 pm

Call to Dinner

Guests are invited to take their seats for the wedding dinner, followed by speeches from the father of the bride, best man, and the groom.

9:00 pm

First Dance

The band takes to the stage as Jemma & Rory take their first dance as husband and wife. Evening guests are welcome from this point, a night of dancing awaits!

10:00 pm

Sigature Afters

A late-night buffet is served to keep everyone fuelled for the dancefloor. The bar remains open and the music plays on until the early hours.

Sunday 20th September — 9:00 am

Morning Breakfast

Join us for a leisurely breakfast at the Lough Erne Resort before you head home. A lovely way to round off the celebrations with family and friends over the beautiful lough.
